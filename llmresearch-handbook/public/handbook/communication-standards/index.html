4. Communication Standards

Effective communication is vital to our success, particularly in a remote environment.

4. Communication Standards

4.1 Asynchronous Communication

Asynchronous communication supports thoughtful responses and detailed documentation.

  • Documentation: Create detailed documents for complex topics.
  • Response Time: Aim to reply within 24 hours.
  • Clarity: Use headings, bullet points, and summaries for clarity.

4. Communication Standards

4.2 Synchronous Communication

Real-time communication is essential for immediate collaboration.

  • Instant Messaging Etiquette: Keep messages concise; use threads for different topics.
  • Video Calls: Test equipment beforehand and mute when not speaking.
  • Availability Indicators: Update your status to reflect your availability.

4. Communication Standards

4.3 Communication Etiquette

Maintaining professional communication is critical.

  • Professional Language: Use clear, formal language.
  • Feedback: Use “I” statements and focus on behaviors rather than personalities.
  • Conflict Resolution: Address issues directly and seek mutual understanding.

4. Communication Standards

4.4 Language and Inclusivity

Our communication should be inclusive and accessible.

  • Inclusive Language: Use gender-neutral terms and avoid assumptions.
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Be aware of cultural differences.
  • Avoid Jargon: Use language that is clear to all colleagues.
