5. Onboarding Process

Our onboarding process is designed to integrate you smoothly into the company.

5. Onboarding Process

5.1 First Day

Your first day sets the tone for your journey with us.

  • Welcome Orientation: Introduction to company culture and values.
  • System Access: Ensure all accounts and tools are set up.
  • Policy Agreements: Review and acknowledge key policies.

5. Onboarding Process

5.2 First Week

Your first week helps you integrate with your team and understand your role.

  • Meet Your Team: One-on-one meetings with team members.
  • Training Sessions: Complete mandatory compliance and security training.
  • Initial Tasks: Begin introductory projects.

5. Onboarding Process

5.3 First Month

The first month is about setting expectations and getting to know the company.

  • Performance Goals: Establish short-term objectives with your manager.
  • Feedback Sessions: Regular check-ins to discuss progress and questions.
  • Company Familiarity: Participate in cross-departmental meetings to understand broader operations.

5. Onboarding Process

5.4 Probationary Period

The probationary period is used to evaluate performance and cultural fit.

  • Duration: Typically 90 days from the start date.
  • Evaluation Criteria: Performance, cultural fit, and teamwork.
  • Outcome: Confirmation of employment or extension if necessary.
